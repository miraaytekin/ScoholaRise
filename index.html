ScholaRise – Connecting Dreams to Resources

Specialized loans and financial guidance for International Baccalaureate (IB) students in Europe.

We help IB students in Germany, Switzerland and the Netherlands finance tuition, living expenses, exam fees and application costs through accessible student loans and personalized consulting.

How It Works

From first questions to final applications, ScholaRise supports you step by step.

  1. Explore your options – Use our Financial Aid Assessment Tool to understand your funding needs and eligibility.
  2. Get personalized guidance – Book a session with our consultants to design the best funding plan for your IB path.
  3. Apply with confidence – Use the Application Management System to submit and track funding applications on time.
  4. Learn & grow – Access our Resource Hub to stay informed throughout your IB journey.

Our Mission & Vision

Vision Statement

“To create a world where every IB student has equal access to quality education, financial support, and the opportunity to reach their full academic and professional potential.”

Mission Statement

“Our mission is to empower IB students by providing affordable student loans, personalized financial guidance, and educational consulting services. We aim to remove financial barriers, support informed decision‑making, and enable students to pursue higher education with confidence and independence.”

Who We Serve

ScholaRise is designed for International Baccalaureate Diploma Programme (IBDP) students and their families.

  • IBDP students planning to study in Europe, especially in Germany, Switzerland and the Netherlands.
  • Parents & guardians seeking clear, responsible and affordable funding options.
  • Schools & partners looking to provide additional financial support pathways for their IB communities.
