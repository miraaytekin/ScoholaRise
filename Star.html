Context

Why Cities Need Play

Urbanisation is slowly killing the soul out of our cities, endangering children’s playgrounds. STEEPLE analysis shows both urgent challenges and powerful opportunities.

Social Urbanisation reduces free, safe play spaces, putting children’s wellbeing at risk.
Technological Advances in eco-friendly, city-compatible playground technology make compact builds possible.
Economic Municipal funding, CSR budgets, and developer investments create strong opportunities, even as downturns may affect cash flow.
Environmental Recycled materials, climate concerns, and green policies create demand for eco-friendly play spaces.
Political & Legal Government priorities around child welfare and green urban planning, plus strict safety and environmental standards, shape how we design and build.
Ethical Commitment to safety, inclusivity, and equitable access builds community trust and long-term partnerships.
